Disease Prevention

Malaria Prevention

  • Use mosquito nets every night.
  • Remove standing water around your home.
  • Wear long sleeves and use insect repellent when outdoors.

COVID-19 Precautions

  • Wash your hands regularly with soap.
  • Wear masks in crowded places.
  • Maintain physical distance whenever possible.

Safe Water and Hygiene

  • Always drink boiled or treated water.
  • Wash fruits and vegetables before eating.
  • Keep your living areas clean and free from garbage.
